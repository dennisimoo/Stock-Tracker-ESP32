Stock Tracker ESP32 Installer

Turn your ESP32 into a live stock display. Shows real-time prices for popular stocks on your screen. Updates automatically and connects to WiFi.

Installation

Install

  1. Connect your ESP32 to your computer with a USB cable.
  2. Click the 'Connect' button below. Use Chrome or Edge browser.
  3. Choose "USB Serial" when asked.
  4. Click 'Install' and wait for it to finish.
Your browser doesn't support Web Serial. Please use Chrome, Edge, or Opera. HTTPS required for Web Serial. Please use a web server.

Installation only works in Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge. WiFi will be configured after setup.
